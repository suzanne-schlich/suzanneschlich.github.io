I am a post-doc at the Department of Mathematics Giuseppe Peano of the Università di Torino under the mentorship of Andrea Seppi, funded by the ERC GENERATE. Before that, I was a post-doc at the Institut Fourier of the Université Grenoble Alpes and I did my PhD at IRMA in Strasbourg and at the Laboratoire Paul Painlevé in Lille. My thesis advisor was François Guéritaud.
Here is my CV.

My main interests include hyperbolic geometry, character varieties and the action of the mapping class group, (higher) Teichmüller theory and more generally geometric structures. In my thesis, I studied primitive-stable and Bowditch representations.
